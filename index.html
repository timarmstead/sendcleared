How it works

Four steps from draft to approved

SendCleared plugs into your existing workflow. No new tools to learn — just add your SendCleared address to your ESP test list and you're set up forever.

1

Send your test email

In your ESP — Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Campaign Monitor — hit "send test" as normal. Address it to your SendCleared inbox instead.

yourname@sendcleared.com

2

Get your QA report

SendCleared receives the email, decodes it, and runs 14 automated checks. Your report is ready in about 10 seconds — pass, warning, or critical per item.

3

Fix and re-run

Fix any flagged issues in your ESP, re-send the test, and re-run. Repeat until you're happy. The full history is saved for every campaign.

4

Send the approval link

Click "Request approval". SendCleared generates a unique magic link. Your client clicks it, views the email, and approves in one tap — no login required.

sendcleared.com/r/xyz789

FAQ

Common questions

Does my client need to create an account? ▾
No — that's the whole point. The approval link is passwordless. Your client clicks the link, views the email, and taps Approve or Request Changes. Nothing to sign up for, no app to download.
Which ESPs does SendCleared work with? ▾
Any ESP that has a "send test email" feature — which is all of them. Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Campaign Monitor, Brevo, HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and more. You add your SendCleared address to the test recipients list once. It works on every campaign from then on.
What's a client inbox address? ▾
Each of your clients gets a unique email address — something like moneyspider@yourname.sendcleared.com. When you add that address to your ESP test list and hit "send test", SendCleared intercepts the email, runs the QA checks, and makes it available in your dashboard. One address per client, set up once, works forever.
How is SendCleared different from Litmus or Email on Acid? ▾
Litmus and Email on Acid are self-serve platforms built around rendering screenshots across 90+ email clients. They're powerful but expensive ($500+/mo for Litmus after its 2025 price hike) and built for large in-house teams. SendCleared is built for freelancers and small agencies who need a quick, affordable pre-send check and a professional client approval workflow — not a full-time testing platform.
Is my email content stored? ▾
Yes — SendCleared stores the email content and QA report so you have a full history per client and campaign. Approval records are stored for your audit trail. You can delete campaign data at any time from your dashboard. We never share your content with third parties.
Can I white-label the approval page? ▾
Yes, on the Agency plan. You can add your own agency logo to the approval page header so clients see your branding, not SendCleared's. The footer "Powered by SendCleared" line is also removable on Solo and above.
